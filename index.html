Flood Alerts Canterbury


This website enables you to check flood levels near you but also the severity of the flood and whether you need to be concerned or not. On our interactive map, green pointers represent sensor values on the Great Stour that are deemed "OK". Red pointers represent that the sensors are picking up some form of flood alert.
